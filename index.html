About Us

Welcome to Visit and Explore Afghanistan (VEA) – your trusted gateway to discovering the hidden beauty, rich culture, and unmatched hospitality of Afghanistan.

Based in Afghanistan, VEA is a passionate and experienced tour company committed to providing safe, authentic, and unforgettable travel experiences for both local and international visitors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to show the world the real Afghanistan – a land of breathtaking landscapes, ancient history, and resilient spirit. Whether you're an adventurer seeking remote mountain treks, a history enthusiast exploring centuries-old monuments, or a cultural traveler eager to connect with local traditions, we design personalized tours tailored to your interests.

What We Offer

Customized Tours Across Afghanistan

From the towering Hindu Kush mountains to the cultural treasures of Herat, the vibrant streets of Kabul to the serene valleys of Bamiyan – we organize tours across the country.

Experienced Local Guides

Our multilingual guides are deeply knowledgeable about Afghanistan’s culture, history, and geography, ensuring a safe and enriching experience.

Comfortable Transportation

We provide private, well-maintained vehicles for all tours, including convenient airport pickup and drop-off services.

Accommodation & Meals

We arrange comfortable accommodations to suit your budget and offer delicious local meals so you can savor the flavors of Afghan cuisine.

Cultural Experiences

Engage in traditional festivals, handicraft workshops, village visits, and more to truly immerse yourself in Afghan life.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our top priority. We closely monitor local conditions and work with communities and authorities to ensure all itineraries are secure and thoughtfully planned.

Our Commitment

At Visit and Explore Afghanistan, we believe in responsible tourism that supports local communities and promotes a positive, authentic image of Afghanistan to the world.

Let us be your guide to one of the world’s most unique and unforgettable travel destinations.
